About Us

Working toward a world where everyone has access to clean water

Wells of Change is dedicated to addressing the global water crisis by building sustainable water projects in communities that lack access to clean water. Since 2018, we have partnered with local organizations and communities to implement long-lasting solutions that improve health, education, and economic opportunities.

Our approach focuses on community involvement, sustainability, and education to ensure that each project continues to provide clean water for generations to come. We believe that access to clean water is a fundamental human right and the foundation for breaking the cycle of poverty.

Our Impact

See how access to clean water transforms communities

Health Improvement

Communities with access to clean water see up to 80% reduction in waterborne diseases, leading to fewer sick days and lower healthcare costs.

Education Access

When children don't have to spend hours collecting water, school attendance increases by 40%, especially among girls who are often responsible for water collection.

Economic Growth

Access to clean water saves time and improves health, allowing community members to focus on income-generating activities and education.
